Who We Are

About nextECA

nextECA is an open-source initiative by nextPrtnr, designed to democratize access to extracurricular opportunities for students worldwide — especially in underrepresented regions like Bangladesh.

Our Mission

We believe every student, regardless of geography or background, deserves access to information about global opportunities. From a student in Sylhet to one in São Paulo — nextECA exists to level the playing field.

By aggregating hackathons, olympiads, scholarships, fellowships, and competitions in one open platform, we reduce the information gap that holds back millions of talented students.

From Our Founders

"We started nextECA after realizing that students in Bangladesh were missing incredible international opportunities simply because they didn't know about them. Information shouldn't be a privilege — it should be a right. nextECA is our attempt to fix that."

— The nextPrtnr Team
