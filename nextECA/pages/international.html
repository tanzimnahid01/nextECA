Why Go International?

Participating in international competitions and programs does more than add a line to your CV. It exposes you to different problem-solving approaches, builds a global network, and demonstrates to universities and employers that you can compete on a world stage.

Many international programs are fully funded — covering travel, accommodation, and meals. Cost is rarely the barrier. Information is. That's why we built nextECA.
